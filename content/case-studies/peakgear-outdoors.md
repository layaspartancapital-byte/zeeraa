# PeakGear Outdoors: 12 Platforms, $2.1M First Year

## The Challenge

PeakGear Outdoors had a strong product line of camping and hiking gear selling exclusively on Amazon. They knew other platforms had potential but lacked the expertise and bandwidth to launch and manage multiple channels simultaneously.

## The Strategy

Zeeraa developed a phased multi-platform expansion plan:

### Phase 1: Core Marketplaces (Months 1-3)
- Walmart Marketplace launch with WFS enrollment
- eBay store buildout targeting outdoor enthusiasts
- Shopify DTC store with adventure-lifestyle branding

### Phase 2: Specialty & Retail (Months 4-6)
- Target Plus marketplace onboarding
- Google Shopping and free listings optimization
- Facebook and Instagram Shop integration

### Phase 3: Expansion (Months 7-8)
- Best Buy marketplace for tech-forward outdoor gear
- Pinterest shopping for discovery-based traffic
- Additional specialty outdoor platforms

## The Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Active Platforms | 1 | 12 |
| Monthly Revenue | $85K | $175K |
| Year 1 Multi-Platform Revenue | $0 | $2.1M |
| Customer Acquisition Cost | $18 | $12 |

PeakGear's diversified platform strategy also reduced their Amazon dependency from 100% to 48%, dramatically reducing business risk.
