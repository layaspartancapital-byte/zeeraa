# NovaBright Beauty: 340% Revenue Growth in 6 Months

## The Challenge

NovaBright Beauty had a quality skincare line but flat Amazon sales hovering around $45K/month. Their listings lacked A+ Content, PPC campaigns were poorly structured, and they had zero presence on other platforms. They needed a partner to unlock their growth potential.

## The Strategy

Zeeraa implemented a comprehensive multi-platform strategy:

### Amazon Optimization
- Complete listing overhaul with keyword research identifying 200+ untapped search terms
- A+ Content design showcasing ingredient science and before/after results
- PPC restructure: built 47 campaigns across Sponsored Products, Brands, and Display
- Launched Brand Store with educational content driving 15% higher conversion

### Shopify DTC Launch
- Built custom Shopify store with subscription model
- Email marketing integration driving 35% repeat purchase rate
- Google Shopping and Meta Ads for DTC acquisition

### TikTok Shop Expansion
- Launched with 50+ affiliate creators in beauty niche
- First live shopping event generated $28K in 2 hours
- Achieved $120K/month within 90 days of launch

## The Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Monthly Revenue | $45K | $198K |
| Amazon ACOS | 42% | 22% |
| Platforms | 1 | 3 |
| Organic Rankings (Top 10) | 8 | 34 |
| Monthly Orders | 1,200 | 5,800 |

NovaBright Beauty now generates $2.4M in annual revenue across three platforms, with Amazon accounting for 55%, Shopify DTC at 25%, and TikTok Shop at 20%.
