# FreshPaws Pet Co: $0 to $850K/Month on TikTok Shop

## The Challenge

FreshPaws Pet Co had a solid Amazon business ($200K/month) selling premium pet supplements and accessories. They'd seen competitors blow up on TikTok Shop but had no social commerce experience, no creator relationships, and no live shopping infrastructure.

## The Strategy

### TikTok Shop Launch (Month 1-2)
- Complete TikTok Shop setup with optimized product catalog
- Created sample kits for 200+ pet influencer outreach
- Set competitive 20% affiliate commission rates
- Launched with 15 products across supplements, toys, and grooming

### Affiliate Scaling (Month 3-6)
- Built network of 150+ active pet content creators
- Top 10 affiliates driving 60% of revenue
- Bi-weekly product drops keeping content fresh
- UGC content repurposed for TikTok Ads

### Live Shopping Program (Month 4-8)
- Weekly live shopping events with professional hosts
- Average live session: $35K in sales
- Best single live: $127K in 4 hours
- Built dedicated live shopping audience of 45K followers

## The Results

| Metric | Month 1 | Month 12 |
|--------|---------|----------|
| TikTok Shop Revenue | $8K | $850K |
| Active Affiliates | 15 | 150+ |
| Monthly Orders | 200 | 22,000 |
| Average Order Value | $42 | $38 |
| Affiliate Content Pieces | 30 | 800+ |

FreshPaws Pet Co's TikTok Shop success also lifted their Amazon sales by 25% as brand awareness increased. Total annual run rate across all channels now exceeds $10.2M.
